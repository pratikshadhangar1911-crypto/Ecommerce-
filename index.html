What is E-Commerce?

E-Commerce, or electronic commerce, is the buying and selling of goods or services online. It allows businesses to reach customers globally without physical stores. Customers can shop conveniently anytime, compare prices, read reviews, and get home delivery. E-Commerce helps businesses reduce operational costs, manage inventory efficiently, and personalize marketing strategies. Online payment gateways, secure transactions, and tracking systems make shopping fast and safe. Modern E-Commerce platforms include websites and mobile apps that serve millions of users worldwide.
